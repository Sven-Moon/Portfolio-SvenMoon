Project Overview

SpellWell performs a comparative analysis of calculated hit percentages between spell saving throw types. Do I use the constitution-save spell or the intelligence-save spell? For a simple tabletop fantasy game, there's a surprising amount of variables that goes into optimizing that answer. This app seeks to pull some of those variables together and come up with an actual answer to "Which is the best spell?"

A quick note: for most people, this directly detracts from the fun of the game. But for analytically-minded people like me, that doesn't matter. The fun is not in vanquishing foes, but in finding the high point in the graph.

SpellWell's Future

  • I'll set up authentication pretty quickly here so people can save their characters and develop a log of monsters they know.
  • The spell set is from a limited list. I'll upgrade it to include the full set in the future once I process the spells provided by 5e.tools
  • Above I state that I use some of the variables, which is true. There are also conditions that can be applied to both the hero and the enemy that can affect hit chance. I'll add those, too.
  • The looks to functionality of this app is skewed toward functionality. The site could use a face lift and I'll provide it at some point.

A final note: If none of this makes sense to you, you're probably neither a numbers nor RPG geek. That's okay. That probably makes you more normal rather than less.
