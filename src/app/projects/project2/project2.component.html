This is why.

Project Overview

SpellWell performs a comparative analysis of calculated hit percentages between spell types. Do I use the constitution-save spell or the intelligence-save spell? For a simple tabletop fantasy game, there's a surprising amount of variables that goes into optimizing that answer. This app seeks to pull all of those variables together and come up with an actual answer to "Which is the best spell?"

A quick note: for most people, this directly detracts from the fun of the game. But for analytically-minded people like me, that doesn't matter. The fun is not in vanquishing foes, but in finding the high point in the graph.

SpellWell's Future

I'll set up authentication pretty quickly here so people can save their characters and develop a log of monsters they know. The posted image shows a 'Spell List', but that doesn't exist right now. There are hundreds of D&D spells, so I'll have to rely on an API to provide them and I just haven't had the time to connect to one yet. I'll also use the API to give players the opportunity to just choose the monster and have its stats filled in.

A final note: If none of this makes sense to you, you're probably neither a numbers nor RPG role playing geek. That's okay. That probably makes you more normal rather than less.
